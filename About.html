Logo Artist

The initial inspiration of MoonShock was actually from a game I play often. Specifically, I envisioned a logo would have the same impact and feel, but with a twist of mystic. The moon is often referred to as such, and having it be suddenly torn apart by a flash of lightning would help get that message across. The logo represents the beauty of the night, the mysterious nature of the moon, the sudden jolt of energy, and if anything else, it's just really cool looking.
